# Code Of Conduct

English | [中文](#社区行为准则)

MobileClaw welcomes practical, respectful collaboration. The project involves device control, screen content, local files, and network behavior, so contributors should be precise and careful when discussing bugs and risks.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and technical without being hostile.
- Keep criticism focused on code, behavior, documentation, and reproducibility.
- Assume Android behavior may differ across devices and ROMs.
- Share enough detail for others to reproduce issues.
- Remove private data from screenshots, logs, proxy configs, and recordings before posting.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private user data, credentials, screenshots, proxy configs, messages, or logs without consent.
- Encouraging hidden telemetry, credential collection, or unsafe device control.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions with unrelated promotion or spam.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may lead to removal from the project community.

# 社区行为准则

[English](#code-of-conduct) | 中文

MobileClaw 欢迎务实、尊重他人的协作。这个项目涉及设备控制、屏幕内容、本地文件和网络行为，因此讨论 Bug 和风险时需要保持准确和谨慎。

## 期望行为

- 直接讨论技术问题，但不要攻击他人。
- 批评应聚焦代码、行为、文档和可复现性。
- 默认理解 Android 行为可能因设备和 ROM 而不同。
- 提供足够细节，帮助别人复现问题。
- 发布截图、日志、代理配置和录屏前，先移除隐私数据。

## 不可接受行为

- 骚扰、人身攻击或歧视性语言。
- 未经同意发布用户私有数据、凭据、截图、代理配置、消息或日志。
- 鼓励隐藏遥测、凭据收集或不安全的设备控制。
- 反复用无关推广或垃圾信息干扰技术讨论。

## 执行

维护者可以编辑、隐藏或删除违反本准则的评论和 Issue。反复或严重违反者可能会被移出项目社区。
